The Burning Moon

It was a dark and stormy night...
The air warm, yet crisp. Thunder rolling in the distant skies.
A young boy holding an oil lamp walks into a secluded town.
His loyal dog Tony walking cautiously by his side.
They approach a dark and worn down house with boarded up windows. Thunder roars in the distance and rain falls. Tony the dog keeps up his guard.
Though they are both getting soaked, they walk, not run, around the side through the black iron bar gate.
